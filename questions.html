Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Visit our Order page, fill out the form with your contact info and the items you'd like, and submit your request. We'll confirm availability and follow up with pickup details.

When can I pick up my order?

Pickup is available Friday through Sunday. After we confirm your order, we'll arrange a specific time that works for you.

Where can I see what is ready?

Check the Fresh Produce page for current prices, produce that is ready to order, and crops that are coming soon.

Is my order confirmed when I submit the form?

Not yet. Your form is an order request. We will review the harvest and contact you to confirm the items, total, and pickup details.

Do you deliver?

We currently offer pickup only. Delivery may be available in the future—stay tuned!

How can I support the garden?

Visit our Support the Garden page to learn how you can contribute. Every bit helps with seeds, soil, and seasonal upkeep.
